Beautiful Sunday Challenge - NEW RULES
美丽星期天行动 - 新规则

I started the #beautifulsunday challenge hoping to people share beautiful story on sunday with at least 2 images or a video with the intent to reward the post with beautiful story which are having low reward. Here are the rules:
  • Post must be posted on Sunday and include the #beautifulsunday tag OR posted via [Sunday] community at https://hive.blog/trending/hive-191668.
  • Post must have at least 2 colour images. Why? Because Monochrome is for Monday.
  • Post must have content that make me feel you are telling a beautiful story in the form of text or video.
  • The story does not have to be something you do on Sunday but if it is, then it will be good if you mentioned that somehow.
  • If you can pop in a link to your #beautifulsunday post into my #beautifulsunday post, it will ensure I don't miss your post.
  • Between Tuesday to Saturday day I will randomly choose up to 3 winners I feel like have told me a beautiful enough story to give them free HIVE.
  • I shall not limit the number of posts but I shall try to choose posts which have low reward before payout. Why? Because if your payout is reward is high, you already have reasonably.
  • The total prize pool for each week is 0.9 HIVE and the prize will be shared by up to 3 winners.
  • If there are no participants for the particular week, I shall not rollover the prize to the following week for simplicity.
  • For a start also, I am funding this #beautifulsunday challenge with HIVE from my own account. If there is anyone who wants to donate, they are most welcome and I can increase the number of winners and/or number of HIVE given to each winner. Otherwise, you can always choose your own #beautifulsunday winners and send them HIVE yourself. :-)
